Welcome to EchoVault 🔊

EchoVault turns any sound into live visuals — and quietly builds a little library of how your music sounds, so you can look back on it later.

  1. 🎵
    Pick a source. Play a music file, use your microphone, or capture this device's own sound.
  2. 🎨
    Watch it react. Switch between visual modes — Bars, Particles, Bloom and more. They dance to the beat.
  3. 💾
    Save a snapshot. Hit Record to store a "fingerprint" of a track. Your vault grows, and you can compare how songs sound across plays and devices.

Reopen this anytime with the ⓘ button up top. No account needed — everything stays on your device.

⚙ Settings

Higher detail is sharper but a touch less snappy. The cutoffs set the loud/quiet range the Meter shows. Changes apply instantly and are remembered.
